Rare Animals of the World

There are certain animals that are scarcely seen in the world.
This site introduces such rare animals, using the unique character of paper crafts.( Editorial supervison : WWF Japan )

Rare Animals in the World1st. series

Coelacanth
Nicknamed a "living fossil", the coelacanth prehistoric fish is now available as a paper craft model.
African Elephant
A paper craft model of the elephant, the largest living land mammal.
Yellow-eyed Penguin
Yamaha has created a paper craft model of the peculiar yellow-eyed penguin, which builds its nest in forests and thickets.
Gorilla
Try making a paper craft of the gorilla, which has a gentle nature in spite of its intimidating appearance.
Great Indian Rhinoceros
Yamaha has captured the essence of the brawny great Indian rhinoceros in a paper craft model.
California Condor
Features the California Condor, a predatory bird in danger of extinction.
Tiger
A paper craft of the tiger, characterized by its fearless appearance and unique stripe pattern.

Rare Animals in the World2nd. series

Sumatran Orang-utan
A paper craft depicting the SUMATRAN ORANG-UTAN, said to be the closest relative of humans.
Dalmatian Pelican
Here is a paper craft of the Dalmatian Pelican whose trademark is a large beak.
Malayan Tapir
Try making this paper craft of the Malayan Tapir who is rumored to eat your bad dreams.
Maned Sloth
Yamaha features the quiet and docile Maned Sloth relaxing at the tops of trees.
Cuban Crocodile
The Cuban Crocodile has the most temperamental disposition of all crocodiles.
Polar Bear
Yamaha presents the polar bear, one of the largest meat eating beasts.
Cape Penguin
Yamaha has created a paper craft of the Cape Penguin, which inhabits the southern tip of Africa.

Rare Animals in the World3rd. - 4th. series

Grevy's Zebra
A beautifully striped Grevy’s zebra.
Little Spotted Kiwi
A Little Spotted Kiwi with a round body and a long beak.
Ring-tailed Lemur
A Ring-tailed Lemur with big round eyes, a long tail, and a unique ring-pattern.
South African Hedgehog
A South African Hedgehog covered with distinctive quills.
Marine Iguana
A Marine Iguana with a jagged crest and a long tail.
Giant Panda
A Giant Panda with its adorable appearance and comical behaviors.
Okapi
Enjoy the paper craft of the Okapi, with very unique stripes on the body.
Steller's Sea Eagle
Steller’s sea eagle, known in Japan as “O-washi” is the largest bird of prey with large wings and a sharp beak.
Juan Fernandez Fur Seal
A slender body distinguishes the Fernandez fur seal from other species of the seal family.
Lion
The lionÍs regal appearance is truly that of the king of beasts.

Rare Animals in the Amazon

Bush Dog
The Bush Dog has a distinct look with a long, stocky body and short legs.
Amazon River Dolphin
The Amazon River Dolphin, easily distinguished by its pink-colored body, is now available as a paper craft model.
Yellow Footed Tortoise
The Yellow Footed Tortoise, well-named for its yellow-scaled limbs, is now available as a paper craft model.
Giant Armadillo
The “giant armadillo” looks as if its whole body is covered with hard armor.
Hyacinth Macaw
A cobalt blue-bodied Hyacinth Macaw has joined the papercraft lineup.
